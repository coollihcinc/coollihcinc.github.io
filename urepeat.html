Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I start using URepeat?
Getting started with URepeat is easy! Just download the app from the App Store, create an account, and follow the on-screen instructions to set up your profile.
How can I sync my data with Apple Health Kit?
To sync your data, go to the Settings in the URepeat app, find the 'Health Sync' option, and follow the prompts to allow data sharing.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Make sure your app is updated if the app is crashing or freezing.
  • Check your internet connection for any syncing issues.
  • Verify the data inputs for accuracy if you are getting inaccurate data readings.
